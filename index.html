CRYSTAL COLLECTOR!

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

There are four crystals above.By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes aboove the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts,the game will change the value of each crystal.
